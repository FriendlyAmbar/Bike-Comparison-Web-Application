The 2014 Harley-Davidson Dyna Fat Bob FXDF model is referred to as a Dark Custom cruiser with a big motorcycle
feel. And with its rider in an aggressive position, the Fat Bob strikes a menacing pose on city streets. The 
Fat Bob is for bikers who want to cruise on the open highways in style and comfort. With meaty tyres, twin 
headlamps and Tommy Gun pipes, the Harley excludes a 'don't mess with me attitude'.The seat gets a 
perforated cover, silver contrast stitching and looks sporty and comfortable. It comes with a five-inch 
'multi-function' LCD display for a speedometer and the LED tail lamps. Powered by a twin-cylinder 1583cc engine
that produces close to 94bhp the bike is available in a matte paint scheme apart from the standard colours.
